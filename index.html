About Us

Agappe Middle East LLC (AME) an ethical & conscientious organisation Established in 2008 has gathered experience in the in-vitro diagnostics industry and has built a business with understanding at its core. Agappe has become synonymous with commitment, flexibility, reliability and quality focused on customer centric, service oriented approach and earned its dividend in the form of strong customer relations. Our high level of flexibility and the strength of our team enables us to adapt quickly to the changing healthcare sector.

AME has developed a number of partnerships with Diagnostics manufacturers in order to supply our customers with complete, cost-effective and innovative solutions. With the comprehensive range of Clinical chemistry, Hematology, Immunology, Dermatology, Medical etc AME has justified the patient satisfaction who is the ultimate beneficiary.
